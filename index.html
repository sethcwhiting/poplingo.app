About PoP Lingo

PoP Lingo is a cutting-edge language learning app that allows you to practice conversing in languages you've already been learning. Engage in interactive conversations with our AI robot, listen to questions asked in your selected language, and speak your answers back for instant feedback.

Key Features

  • Interactive Conversations with AI Robot
  • Listening and Speaking Exercises
  • Language Assistance and Translations
  • Adjustable Speaking Speed
  • Verb Conjugation Chart
